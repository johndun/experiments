## Mathematical Formulas

Here are some example equations rendered with MathJax.

### Inline Math

The quadratic formula is $x = \frac{-b \pm \sqrt{b^2 - 4ac}}{2a}$.

Einstein's famous equation $E = mc^2$ relates energy and mass.

### Block Equations

The Gaussian integral:

$$\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} e^{-x^2} dx = \sqrt{\pi}$$

Euler's identity:

$$e^{i\pi} + 1 = 0$$

The Navier-Stokes equation for incompressible flow:

$$\rho \left( \frac{\partial \mathbf{v}}{\partial t} + \mathbf{v} \cdot \nabla \mathbf{v} \right) = -\nabla p + \mu \nabla^2 \mathbf{v} + \mathbf{f}$$

### Summations and Limits

The Taylor series for $e^x$:

$$e^x = \sum_{n=0}^{\infty} \frac{x^n}{n!}$$

The definition of a derivative:

$$f'(x) = \lim_{h \to 0} \frac{f(x+h) - f(x)}{h}$$
